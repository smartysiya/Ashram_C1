Universal Welfare

PRYYAS Trust

PRYYAS stands for Environment Protection, Yoga, Yajna, and Ayurveda Conservation Trust (Prakruti Rakshan, Yoga, Yagya, Ayurveda Sanrakshan). Founded by Her Holiness Devi Maa Shivanginand Giri Ji, the trust operates from Bhopal and is dedicated to holistic well-being and universal harmony.

  • Environment: Reforestation and nature conservation.
  • Yoga: Physical and mental harmony.
  • Yajna: Spiritual purification through sacred fire.
  • Ayurveda: Traditional healing and natural lifestyle.
