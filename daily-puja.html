About Our Daily Rituals

The Aadi Shakti Devi Maa Ashram conducts daily authentic Vedic rituals to invoke the blessings of the Divine Mother. These ceremonies are performed by our highly trained resident priests using pure ingredients and strict adherence to traditional scriptures.

Participating in or witnessing these daily pujas helps purify the mind, cleanse spiritual blockages, and brings immense peace and prosperity to devotees.
