The Philosophy of Inner Union

Yoga is not just a physical practice; it is the journey of the self, through the self, to the self. At the Aadi Shakti Ashram, we teach traditional Hatha Yoga and extensive guided Meditation specifically aimed at Kundalini awakening and spiritual balance.

We welcome seekers of all levels—whether you are looking to find simple mental peace away from the chaotic world or seeking deep spiritual enlightenment.
